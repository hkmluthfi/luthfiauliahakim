About Me

I am an observer by nature. While others speak, I listen—to people, to environments, and to data. As a final-year Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering student at Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), with an enriching academic exchange at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), I channel this quiet focus into the meticulous world of spatial analysis.

My work revolves around translating real-world physical environments into actionable insights. During my time as a Field Operation Intern at PT Anugerah Samudera Madanindo, I gained firsthand experience observing survey operations and how massive port development projects are executed at Patimban Port. I find beauty in precision, clarity, and the structured coordination required within complex systems.

Looking ahead, I am passionate about bringing spatial intelligence and structured coordination into dynamic industries. Here are the core sectors and disciplines I am actively expanding my expertise in:

Geographic Information System

Translating complex spatial data into clear, interactive digital maps.

Project Management

Ensuring strategic coordination, efficiency, and structured execution.

Oil & Gas Sector

Applying geospatial intelligence to optimize energy industry operations.

Banking Industry

Leveraging analytical thinking for data-driven financial insights.

Hydrography

Conducting marine surveys and mapping underwater topography for coastal development.

Land Surveying

Acquiring precise terrestrial spatial data to support infrastructure and land management.
